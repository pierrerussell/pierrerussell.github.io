Lasalle House Booking System

A comprehensive booking management platform built for a not-for-profit retreat house, handling the complete lifecycle from initial enquiry through to post-stay analytics.

This project is in active production use. Source code is private to protect organisational data.

Project Overview

The Challenge

A not-for-profit retreat house needed to modernise their booking operations. They were managing residential accommodations, meeting facilities, and meal services using a combination of spreadsheets and paper-based systems. This led to:

  • Double bookings and scheduling conflicts
  • Inconsistent pricing across similar bookings
  • No visibility into conversion rates or booking trends
  • Difficulty tracking guest requirements and dietary needs

The Solution

A purpose-built booking system that unifies all aspects of retreat house operations:

  • Single source of truth for all bookings and enquiries
  • Automated quote generation with configurable pricing rules
  • Real-time availability and occupancy dashboards
  • Secure customer forms for gathering requirements
  • Comprehensive audit trail for compliance

Key Architecture Decisions

Clean Architecture

The backend follows clean architecture principles with separate layers for Domain, Application, and Infrastructure. This enables easy testing and future modifications without affecting the core business logic.

Event-Driven Status Changes

Booking status transitions trigger domain events that automatically handle side effects like sending emails, updating analytics, and creating audit entries. This keeps the core booking logic clean and predictable.

Secure One-Time Links

Customer form links use cryptographically secure tokens with configurable expiry. This allows external data collection without requiring customer accounts, reducing friction in the booking process.

Comprehensive Audit Trail

All data changes are logged with before/after values, user context, and timestamps. Audit logs are append-only and cannot be modified or deleted, ensuring compliance with organisational requirements.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge

Mixed booking types: Some guests stay overnight with meals, others attend day events only

Solution

Flexible data model where room assignments and meal selections are independent entities. A booking can have any combination of rooms, meeting spaces, and meals without rigid packages.

Challenge

Staggered group arrivals: Group members may arrive on different dates

Solution

Guest records have individual arrival/departure dates separate from the booking dates. Room assignments are tracked per-night, allowing guests to move rooms during their stay.

Challenge

Complex pricing with seasonal rates, group discounts, and custom adjustments

Solution

Rule-based quote engine with configurable rate tables. Payments Controllers can override calculated prices while maintaining full audit trail of all quote versions.
